Ustia

Privacy Policy · Last Updated: April 18, 2026

In short: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, track, or sell any personal information.

1. What We Collect

Ustia is designed around privacy. All your data is processed and stored locally on your device. Here's what we collect:

  • Focus session data — Duration, start/end times, and session labels you create — stored locally on your device only
  • Screen time data — Aggregated app usage statistics — stored locally and never transmitted anywhere
  • App preferences — Settings and customization choices you make within Ustia, stored securely on your device
  • Health data (optional) — If you choose to integrate with Apple Health, we read and write focus and mindfulness data to your Health app. This data never leaves your device
  • Crash logs — Anonymous technical information when the app crashes unexpectedly (used to improve app quality)

2. What We Don't Collect

We explicitly commit to never collecting or accessing:

  • Your personal identity information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Your precise location data
  • Your contacts, calendar, or messages
  • Your activity in other apps
  • Your browsing history or web access records
  • Any data that can personally identify you

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on your device using iOS's built-in storage (UserDefaults and local database). We do not maintain any external servers that hold your personal data.

If you enable iCloud sync through your Apple ID, your data is synced through your personal iCloud account, protected by Apple's end-to-end encryption and governed by Apple's privacy policy.

4. Third-Party Services

Ustia uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Store Connect — For in-app purchases and subscription management (governed by Apple's privacy policy)
  • Apple Health — Optional health data integration (you control this entirely in iOS Settings → Privacy → Health)

We do not share any data with advertising networks, analytics companies, or data brokers.

5. Children's Privacy

Ustia is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including on-device encryption and secure server infrastructure for cloud-synced features.

7. Your Rights

Depending on applicable laws in your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact: privacy@techidaily.com

8. Policy Changes

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the updated version on this page and update the "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated clearly within the app.
