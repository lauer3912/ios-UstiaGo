Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

UstiaGo helps you manage your screen time with complete privacy. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, usage data, device identifiers, or analytics from this app.

  • No personal data is collected
  • No third-party analytics or tracking services are integrated
  • No advertising or marketing SDKs are included
  • No user accounts or registration required

Local Data Storage

All data (such as your screen time records and settings) is stored locally on your device and is under your sole control. We have no access to this data.

Permissions

This app may request the following device permissions, solely for the stated functional purpose:

  • Screen Time (Device Activity) — To read your daily screen time data and display usage statistics
  • App Usage — To show which apps you spend the most time on
  • Notifications — To alert you when you've reached your screen time limits

Third-Party Services

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
